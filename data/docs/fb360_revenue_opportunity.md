# How to add revenue opportunity to a feedback record in FB360

## Overview
Revenue opportunity is the estimated commercial value that could be unlocked if the feedback is acted upon. It is one of the most important fields in FB360 because it translates qualitative client sentiment into a quantifiable business case. Sales and Marketing leads are responsible for entering and maintaining this figure.

## What revenue opportunity means
Revenue opportunity is not the client's current contract value. It represents:
- The incremental revenue that could be generated if the requested feature is built
- The renewal value at risk if the feedback is not addressed
- The expansion opportunity if this feature would enable the client to increase their usage

## Who should enter revenue opportunity
Revenue opportunity figures should be entered or reviewed by:
- Sales leads who have commercial visibility of the client account
- Account managers who understand the client's budget and growth plans
- Marketing leads who have broader market data on feature demand

Support leads should not estimate revenue opportunity figures without input from Sales.

## Step-by-step instructions

### Step 1 — Open the feedback record
Navigate to the feedback record in FB360. Use the Feedback ID or search by client and date.

### Step 2 — Click "Edit"
Click the **Edit** button on the feedback detail page to enter edit mode.

### Step 3 — Locate the Revenue Opportunity field
Scroll to the **Revenue opportunity (USD)** field in the Business Impact section.

### Step 4 — Enter the estimated figure
Enter the revenue opportunity in USD as a whole number. Do not include currency symbols or commas — the system formats the number automatically.

Guidelines for estimating:
- **Feature request that could prevent churn** — enter the client's annual contract value
- **Feature that would enable expansion** — enter the estimated incremental annual value
- **Satisfaction feedback with no commercial implication** — enter 0
- **NPS feedback** — use the client's contract value as a proxy for risk

### Step 5 — Add a business impact description
In the **Business impact description** field, explain the reasoning behind the revenue figure. Include:
- How the figure was calculated or estimated
- Whether the client explicitly mentioned a commercial consequence
- Any relevant context from the account relationship

### Step 6 — Save the record
Click **Save**. The revenue opportunity figure is immediately reflected in any linked feature request's aggregated total.

## Updating revenue opportunity
Revenue opportunity figures should be reviewed and updated:
- After each QBR or business review with the client
- When the client's contract is renewed or expanded
- When market conditions change significantly

## Tips
- A feedback record with a $0 revenue opportunity will have less influence on prioritisation — always try to quantify the commercial impact.
- If you are unsure of the exact figure, enter a conservative estimate and add a note explaining the uncertainty.
- Revenue opportunity figures are visible to engineering and product teams — be accurate and honest.
